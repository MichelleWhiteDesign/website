Hero Box

Goal

Focused on large format printing and assembling a printed product.

Process

Boom, Zap, Pow! A Kleenex box redesign featuring DC comic superheroes Batman and Superman, they are here to rescue you from your worst cold and allergy symptoms. A continuously flowing city and hand drawn characters bring the box to life. Printing the large shape and making sure everything lined up was the greatest challenge but together the heroes came to my rescue.
