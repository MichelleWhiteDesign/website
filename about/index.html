About Me

Hello! My name is Michelle White and I am a graphic designer from Edmonton, Alberta. I have a passion for print design, funky socks and Batman. You can catch me studying the menus at restaurants, or the logos on business signs when driving (while still keeping my eyes on the road of course). My favorite color is #A269AC, but I realize not everything can be purple. I am a graduate from MacEwan University, a small business owner and a zealous hip-hop dancer. I believe that great design is a step in the right direction. Let me help you!
